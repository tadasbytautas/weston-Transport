Welcome to Weston Transport

A Leader In Crane -Mounted Haulage Across The United Kingdom

Weston Transport Limited was formed in 1997 and has enjoyed steady growth ever since. From the original three vehicles based in Glasgow the fleet now consists of 40 vehicles spread across the United Kingdom. The fleet consists of a verity of both vehicle and crane sizes from 23tonne metre 4 wheelers to 85 tonne metre artics and 8 wheelers with drag trailers.

The Weston operation is based on providing Premium Customer Service with a high level of operator competency. The driver pool has a remarkably low turnover, reflecting the fact that only a motivated well-trained workforce can provide the level of customer service which is required both by Weston Transport Management and Customers.

The Company works primarily from depots in Glasgow and Coventry which are both conveniently placed next to the M73 and M6 motorways. It also operates from smaller satellite depots in Inverurie, Livingston and Wolverhampton.

Weston Transports Fleet comprises of 40 vehicles based mainly in Glasgow and Coventry with satellite depots in Inverurie, Livingston and Wolverhampton. The vehicles are mainly DAF, Scania and MAN with Fassi, PM and Putzmeister supplying the truck mounted cranes. The crane sizes span from 23tonne metre to 85 tonne metre and vehicle vary from small 4 wheelers up to both artics and 8 wheelers with drag trailers.

With the increasing complexity of jobs in the truck mounted crane industry all jobs are subject to risk assessments and method statements and all drivers are ALLMI trained to ensure, as far as possible, a safe and smooth operation.

All the best equipment and new vehicles count for nothing if the people are not performing at their best. Weston Transport is committed to the highest standards of training. All drivers are ALLMI trained and the level of staff retention is excellent which has led to a stable, close knit workforce.
