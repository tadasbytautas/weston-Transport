Welcome to Weston Transport

The largest fleet of truck mounted cranes in Scotland.

A leader in the crane-mounted haulage business in central Scotland. The company has grown steadily in the past 14 years under the management of Graeme Sherry. When Graeme acquired the company in 1997, the fleet comprised three HIAB trucks and a flatbed, but now operates a more than 30 vehicles out of two depots.

The Weston Transport operation is based on premium customer service and a high level of operator training. The driver pool has a remarkably low turnover, reflecting the fact that only a motivated and well-trained workforce can provide the level of customer service which Graeme demands.

The company now has bases in Glasgow and Coventry. The Glasgow depot is conveniently located close to junction 2A of the M73, and has ready access to all of the major road networks, - North to South and East to West. The Coventry depot is just as conveniently placed, being only a HIAB reach away from the M6 at Junction 3.

Weston transport operates the largest and most diverse fleet of truck mounted cranes, ranging from 4 wheel rigids with 17 tonne meter cranes, to articulated vehicles with 72 tonne meter cranes.

Weston Transport’s fleet comprises 25 trucks based at Gartcosh, and 5 at Coventry. Of the Gartcosh fleet, there are 23 Scanias, one Volvo and one Hino. This further breaks down into 21 rigid and 4 articulated units. Most vehicles are bought new, and are serviced and maintained by the Scania depot at Bellshill. The cranes are a mixture of Fassi, PM and Palfinger, depending on which best suits the application.

For operators’ and customers’ security, all Weston Transport vehicles are fitted with satellite tracking, and a Tracker security device. With the increasing complexity and regulation of the haulage industry, all jobs are subject to a Risk Assessment and Method Statement, in advance, to ensure a safe and smooth operation.
